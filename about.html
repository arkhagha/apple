I define design as being moved by the way everything else moves.

Spaces, sounds, buildings, images, nature, products. Every thing is a result of iterations of design. Computer science itself started from the jacquard loom. I consider this history to also be an analogy for my personal brand:
tech + design.

My education as a computer science major along my digital media studies certificate at the University of Wisconsin-Madison have been versatile and enriching tools. Thus far, I have been able to engage with a spectrum of medias, ideas, principles, art forms, and technologies. Collectively, these experiences have (& continously) steer my curiosity about emerging tech and their implications.

Through various opportunities I have been able to build skills within engineering, design, data, and marketing. In addition, I have knowledge in product delivery process within scrum and agile implemented enviroments.

I am always eager to explore new opportunities, especially those involving innovation, software development, design thinking, branding, & product vision.
